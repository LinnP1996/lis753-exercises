Real Madrid Players

Sergio Ramos

Sergio Ramos is a Spanish defender who is currently the captain on Real Madrid.

He is the most veteran player on the team havin been in the club since 2005.

Ramos has been considered among the world's best defender for the past decade.

With good leadership skills and lots of experience he is one of Real's best players.

Karim Benzema

Karim Benzema is a French striker currently at his 10th year with Real Madrid.

He as a total of 237 goals for Real Madrid, placing in the top 10 of record goal scorers.

Hard working, patient Benzema has all the qualities of a world class player.

Despite being 31 Real Madrid heavily depend on him to score goals and win games.

Luka Modric

Luka Modric is the engine of Real Madrid's midfield. He dictates play with his technical ability.

Despite being the oldest player currently in the club at 34 he is still an important player.

One of his noticeable achievements was helping Croatia reach the 2018 World Cup Final.

In 2018 he was named the best player in the world thanks to his perfomances with Real Madrid and Croatia.
