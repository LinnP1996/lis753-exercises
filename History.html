History of Real Madrid

Real Madrid was founded on March 6th, 1902.

They won their very first trophy in 1909 beating Bilbao.

They established themselves as a major force in the 1950s.

At this time they won many European trophies.

At the turn of the Millenium they appointed Florentino Perez as club President.

Since then he has been an influential figure at the club thanks to his management skills.
